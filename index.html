About & Support
How streaks are counted
A day counts toward your streak once you've marked at least 70% of the day's register complete — you don't need a perfect day, just a consistent one. Miss the threshold and the streak resets, so the Record tab's 12-month view is the fastest way to spot a slipping week before it becomes a lost month.
Study tips
Protect your study slots like fixed appointments — same start time daily builds recall better than longer, irregular sessions. Rotate your weakest subjects into your first slot while focus is freshest, and reserve evenings for revision rather than new topics.
Your data & privacy
Everything you log is saved on this device first. If you sign in, it also syncs to your private cloud account so you can pick up on another device — nobody else can see it.
Getting the most out of the To Do list & Habit Register
The To Do list is for one-off tasks — add and clear them as the day goes. The Habit Register just below tracks recurring daily habits and drives your streak; add your own habits with the "+ Add" row, or remove any you added by tapping the small ✕ that appears on custom habits. Tap the chevron on any card's header to collapse it out of the way — it always reopens the next time you load the app.
Using Focus Mode
Tapping "Start Focus" drops you into a distraction-free full-screen timer with just the countdown and a calm breathing animation. The timer keeps running even if you switch apps, lock your phone, or close the tab — it's based on the actual clock, not a background loop, so it stays accurate. Turn on Notifications in Preferences to get an alert the moment a session or break ends.
Managing subjects
Add, and remove, the subjects you study from the Study Subjects card above. They'll show up in the Pomodoro slot picker and the manual time log on the Pomodoro tab.
Notes tips
Pin important notes to keep them at the top, organize related notes into folders, and export or share any note as plain text from the menu inside the editor.
